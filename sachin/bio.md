# Bio — Sachin

## Short Bio

Sachin Naik is a co-founder of CrispAI and a polyglot technologist specializing in GenAI, LLMs, agent frameworks, and cloud/data infrastructure — particularly in healthcare and high-complexity environments. An IIT Bombay alumnus and serial entrepreneur, he has a track record of building scalable systems across startups and mid-sized teams, with expert-level depth spanning 15+ programming languages from Python and Scala down to x86 and RISC assembly. Most recently he was part of Arcion, acquired by Databricks. He is based in the San Francisco Bay Area.

## Extended Bio

### Background

Sachin has worn every hat in the startup playbook — from solo founder to engineering leader at 10- and 30-person companies, to roles in large corporate environments. His career spans data engineering, ML systems, AI infrastructure, and product leadership. He was part of the Arcion team prior to its acquisition by Databricks, giving him first-hand experience scaling data products to enterprise.

### At CrispAI

CrispAI is an AI company operating across four domains: autonomous AI agents and workflow automation, AI infrastructure and developer tooling, AI-powered SaaS products, and AI consulting and services. The company's differentiating focus is on **on-premises model deployment** — helping organizations run powerful AI without sending data to the cloud — with specializations in **robotics** and **medical AI**.

Sachin co-leads CrispAI alongside co-founders Praveen, Dr. Patrick Damaso, Rohan Pai, Srinivas Dharmasharan, SG Anand, and Sameer Gupta, with advisors Alok Nandan and Rajkumar Sen providing strategic guidance.

### Robotics & Medical AI

Sachin has hands-on expertise in applying AI to robotics systems and medical imaging. He has worked with CT and MRI imaging pipelines, and has been a FIRST Robotics mentor since 2015 — coaching FTC teams in CAD, Java programming, and hardware integration. This real-world robotics background directly informs CrispAI's work in embodied AI and medical diagnostics.

### Philosophy

Sachin believes in continuous learning, mentorship, and building technology that works in the real world — not just in demos. He is particularly passionate about making AI deployable on-premises for industries where data privacy is non-negotiable — healthcare, robotics, and defense. He speaks English, Hindi, Marathi, and German, and has been an ACM Science Fair judge since 2011.

### Patents

Holds two issued patents related to distributed systems and network traffic control.
