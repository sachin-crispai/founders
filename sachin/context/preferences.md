## Work Style

- **Decision making:** Moves fast, data-informed, comfortable with uncertainty

## With AI Agents

- **Preferred response length:** Short and direct; lead with the answer, detail on request
- **Output format:** Structured — bullet points, tables, and code over prose
- **Action style:** Balance of autonomous execution and confirmation for irreversible actions
